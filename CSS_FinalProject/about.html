About Me

My name is Connor Laber, a Denver, Colorado native shaped by the rugged beauty of the Rocky Mountains, where I grew up fishing, hiking, and mountain biking. Armed with two bachelors degrees in Business Administration and Finance., I discovered a deeper calling through coding- a spark ignited when I taught myself HTML, CSS, Python, Javascript, and Swift to build a website to serve the needs of my mothers business, What began as a curious challenge to solve a practical need became a mission to harness technology for good. I dream of leaving a lasting, Christ-centered impact on the world, crafting innovative FinTech and private equity solutions that empower people, simplify lives, and lay the foundation for a brighter future.
